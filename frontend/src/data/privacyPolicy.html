Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2024
Effective Date: January 1, 2024

At RentDrive Private Limited, we are committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining the confidentiality of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data in compliance with the Information Technology Act, 2000, and other applicable Indian data protection laws.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Identity Information: Full name, date of birth, gender, nationality
  • Contact Details: Email address, mobile number, residential address, emergency contact
  • Government Documents: Aadhaar number, PAN card, passport details, voter ID
  • Driving Credentials: License number, issue/expiry dates, endorsements, violation history

1.2 Financial Information

  • Payment Data: Credit/debit card details, bank account information, UPI IDs
  • Transaction History: Booking payments, security deposits, refunds, penalties
  • Credit Information: Credit score checks for premium bookings (with consent)

1.3 Booking and Usage Data

  • Rental History: Vehicle preferences, booking patterns, duration, locations
  • Driving Behavior: GPS tracking data, speed monitoring, route information
  • Feedback: Reviews, ratings, complaints, suggestions

1.4 Technical Information

  • Device Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Usage Analytics: Website/app interactions, search queries, page views
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates, pickup/drop locations, real-time tracking

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Service Provision

  • Processing and managing car rental bookings
  • Identity and driving license verification
  • Payment processing and security deposit management
  • Vehicle allocation and availability management
  • Customer support and assistance

2.2 Safety and Security

  • Real-time vehicle tracking for safety
  • Emergency assistance and roadside support
  • Fraud prevention and risk assessment
  • Compliance with traffic and safety regulations

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Important: We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for commercial purposes.

3.1 Authorized Sharing

  • Service Partners: Payment processors, insurance providers, vehicle maintenance
  • Technology Providers: Cloud hosting, analytics, customer support platforms
  • Verification Services: Identity verification, background check agencies

3.2 Legal Requirements

  • Law enforcement agencies (with valid legal requests)
  • Regulatory authorities (RTO, tax departments)
  • Court orders and legal proceedings
  • Emergency services for safety purposes

4. Data Security Measures

4.1 Technical Safeguards

  • Encryption: AES-256 encryption for data at rest, TLS 1.3 for data in transit
  • Access Controls: Multi-factor authentication, role-based access
  • Network Security: Firewalls, intrusion detection systems, VPN access
  • Regular Audits: Quarterly security assessments and penetration testing

4.2 Organizational Measures

  • Employee training on data protection
  • Confidentiality agreements with staff and partners
  • Incident response and breach notification procedures
  • Regular backup and disaster recovery testing

5. Data Retention Policy

Data Category Retention Period Reason
Account Information Until account deletion + 3 years Legal compliance
Booking Records 7 years Tax and audit requirements
Payment Data As per RBI guidelines Financial regulations
GPS Tracking 90 days Safety and dispute resolution

6. Your Privacy Rights

6.1 Access and Portability

  • Request copies of your personal data
  • Download your data in machine-readable format
  • Receive information about data processing activities

6.2 Correction and Updates

  • Update incorrect or incomplete information
  • Modify your communication preferences
  • Change consent settings

6.3 Deletion and Restriction

  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Restrict processing for specific purposes
  • Object to automated decision-making

7. International Data Transfers

Your data is primarily stored and processed within India. Any international transfers are conducted with appropriate safeguards including adequacy decisions by Indian authorities and standard contractual clauses.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under 18 years. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete the information immediately.

9. Regulatory Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with:

  • Information Technology Act, 2000
  • Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices) Rules, 2011
  • Reserve Bank of India guidelines on data protection
  • Motor Vehicle Act regulations
